    FAQ

    Common questions

    What is a Capacity Clinical Instrument?
    A longitudinal record of your capacity states over 90+ days. Unlike mood scales or symptom checklists, the CCI tracks how you perform under real-world cognitive, emotional, and environmental load. It's designed to be shared with therapists, coaches, and care teams.
    Is this a diagnostic tool?
    No. Orbital is not a medical device and does not diagnose conditions. The CCI is an observational capacity record that supplements clinical judgment.
    What does the founding offer include?
    $199 one-time payment. You get the full CCI report generated after 90 days of daily logging, plus 3 months of Pro access free. This price is locked for the first 100 users only.
    How long does a daily check-in take?
    About 5 seconds. You swipe to set your state and optionally add context tags.
    Who is this for?
    High-performance professionals, neurodivergent adults, therapists tracking client capacity, executive coaches, and sports performance staff.
