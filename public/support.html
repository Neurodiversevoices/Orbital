Data & Privacy

Orbital stores capacity data on-device. If cloud sync is enabled, data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not sell your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Orbital is not a medical device. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. All data is self-reported and subjective. Pattern analysis features identify trends in self-reported data only and do not predict health outcomes. If you have concerns about your health, consult qualified healthcare providers.
