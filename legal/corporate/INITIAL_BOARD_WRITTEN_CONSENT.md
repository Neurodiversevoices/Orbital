# UNANIMOUS WRITTEN CONSENT OF THE SOLE DIRECTOR

## IN LIEU OF THE ORGANIZATIONAL MEETING OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS

## OF

## [CORPORATION NAME], INC.

---

The undersigned, being the sole director of [CORPORATION NAME], Inc., a [State] corporation (the "Corporation"), acting pursuant to the provisions of the Corporation's Bylaws and applicable state law, hereby adopts the following resolutions by unanimous written consent in lieu of an organizational meeting:

---

## 1. ARTICLES OF INCORPORATION

**RESOLVED**, that the filing of the Articles of Incorporation with the Secretary of State of [State] on [Date], and the issuance of a Certificate of Incorporation, are hereby ratified and approved.

---

## 2. BYLAWS

**RESOLVED**, that the Bylaws attached hereto as **Exhibit A** are hereby adopted as the Bylaws of the Corporation.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that the Secretary is hereby authorized and directed to insert the Bylaws in the Corporation's minute book and to see that a copy is kept at the Corporation's principal office.

---

## 3. ELECTION OF OFFICERS

**RESOLVED**, that the following individuals are hereby elected to the offices set forth opposite their respective names, to serve until the first annual meeting of shareholders and until their successors are elected and qualified, or until their earlier resignation or removal:

| Office | Name |
|--------|------|
| President and Chief Executive Officer | [FOUNDER NAME] |
| Secretary | [FOUNDER NAME] |
| Treasurer and Chief Financial Officer | [FOUNDER NAME] |

---

## 4. CORPORATE SEAL

**RESOLVED**, that the seal, an impression of which is affixed to the margin of this Consent, is hereby adopted as the corporate seal of the Corporation.

*[SEAL]*

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that the use of a corporate seal is not required for the valid execution of any document by the Corporation.

---

## 5. FISCAL YEAR

**RESOLVED**, that the fiscal year of the Corporation shall end on December 31 of each year.

---

## 6. PRINCIPAL OFFICE

**RESOLVED**, that the principal office of the Corporation shall be located at:

**Address:** _______________________________________________

**City, State, ZIP:** _______________________________________________

---

## 7. REGISTERED AGENT

**RESOLVED**, that the registered agent and registered office of the Corporation in [State] shall be as set forth in the Articles of Incorporation, or as subsequently changed in accordance with applicable law.

---

## 8. BANK ACCOUNTS

**RESOLVED**, that the officers of the Corporation are hereby authorized to open one or more accounts with such banks, trust companies, or other depositories as the officers may select, and that the officers are authorized to deposit and withdraw funds of the Corporation, execute checks and other instruments, and take all other actions necessary or appropriate in connection with such accounts.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that the officers are hereby authorized to execute and deliver to such banks, trust companies, or depositories such documents, resolutions, and agreements as may be required to open such accounts and conduct banking business.

---

## 9. TAXPAYER IDENTIFICATION NUMBER

**RESOLVED**, that the officers of the Corporation are hereby authorized to apply for a federal employer identification number (EIN) and any state or local tax identification numbers as may be required.

---

## 10. ELECTION OF SUBCHAPTER C STATUS

**RESOLVED**, that the Corporation shall be treated as a Subchapter C corporation for federal income tax purposes.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that the officers are hereby authorized to prepare and file any elections or returns necessary to effectuate the foregoing.

---

## 11. ACCOUNTING METHOD

**RESOLVED**, that the Corporation shall maintain its books and records on the cash or accrual method of accounting, as determined by the Treasurer in consultation with the Corporation's accountants.

---

## 12. QUALIFICATION TO DO BUSINESS

**RESOLVED**, that the officers of the Corporation are hereby authorized to take all actions and file all documents necessary to qualify the Corporation to do business as a foreign corporation in any state or jurisdiction where such qualification is required by law or deemed advisable.

---

## 13. STOCK AUTHORIZATION

**RESOLVED**, that the Corporation is hereby authorized to issue and sell shares of its Common Stock, par value $0.0001 per share (or no par value, as applicable), as authorized by the Articles of Incorporation.

---

## 14. ISSUANCE OF FOUNDER SHARES

**RESOLVED**, that the Corporation is hereby authorized to issue to [FOUNDER NAME] an aggregate of [NUMBER] shares of Common Stock, representing 100% of the authorized and issued shares of the Corporation, for the consideration set forth below:

| Shareholder | Number of Shares | Consideration | Value |
|-------------|------------------|---------------|-------|

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that the consideration received or to be received for such shares is hereby determined to be adequate and that such shares, when issued, shall be fully paid and nonassessable.

---

## 15. FORM OF STOCK CERTIFICATE

**RESOLVED**, that the form of stock certificate attached hereto as **Exhibit B** is hereby approved and adopted as the form of stock certificate for shares of Common Stock of the Corporation.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that the Corporation may issue uncertificated shares of stock in lieu of certificates.

---

## 16. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY ASSIGNMENT

**RESOLVED**, that the officers of the Corporation are hereby authorized to accept an assignment of intellectual property from [FOUNDER NAME] pursuant to an Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement, attached hereto as **Exhibit C**, and to take all actions necessary or appropriate to effect such assignment and protect the intellectual property of the Corporation.

---

## 17. CONFIDENTIALITY AND PROPRIETARY RIGHTS

**RESOLVED**, that all directors, officers, employees, and contractors of the Corporation shall be required to execute a Confidential Information and Invention Assignment Agreement in a form approved by the Board of Directors.

---

## 18. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT AND RATIFICATION

**RESOLVED**, that the Corporation shall reimburse [FOUNDER NAME] for all reasonable expenses incurred in connection with the organization and formation of the Corporation, upon presentation of appropriate documentation.

**RESOLVED FURTHER**, that all actions heretofore taken by the incorporator, directors, and officers of the Corporation in connection with the organization and formation of the Corporation are hereby ratified, approved, and confirmed.

---

## 19. INSURANCE

**RESOLVED**, that the officers of the Corporation are hereby authorized to obtain such insurance policies, including directors and officers liability insurance, general liability insurance, and property insurance, as may be necessary or advisable for the protection of the Corporation and its directors, officers, and employees.

---

## 20. COMPLIANCE WITH SECURITIES LAWS

**RESOLVED**, that the officers of the Corporation are hereby authorized to take all actions necessary to comply with applicable federal and state securities laws in connection with the issuance and sale of securities of the Corporation, including the filing of any notices, exemption filings, or registration statements as may be required.

---

## 21. GENERAL AUTHORIZATION

**RESOLVED**, that the officers of the Corporation are hereby authorized and empowered, in the name and on behalf of the Corporation, to take any and all actions, execute and deliver any and all agreements, instruments, and documents, and to incur such expenses as they deem necessary, advisable, or appropriate to carry out the intent and purposes of the foregoing resolutions.

---

## 22. EFFECTIVE DATE

These resolutions shall be effective as of the date first written below.
