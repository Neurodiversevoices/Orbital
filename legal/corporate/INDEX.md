## MINORITY-OWNED / DISABILITY-OWNED CERTIFICATION PRESERVATION

This document package is designed to preserve eligibility for:

- Minority Business Enterprise (MBE) certification
- Disadvantaged Business Enterprise (DBE) certification
- Disability-Owned Business Enterprise (DOBE) certification
- Woman-Owned Business Enterprise (WBE) certification
- Veteran-Owned Business certification
- HUBZone certification
- 8(a) Business Development Program eligibility

**Key Certification-Preservation Features:**

1. **100% Sole Ownership:** Single founder owns all issued and outstanding stock
2. **Full Control:** Sole founder serves as sole director and all principal officers
3. **No Outside Investors:** No preferred stock issued; no investor board seats
4. **No Dilution:** No option pool or convertible instruments that could dilute ownership
5. **Clear Chain of Title:** IP assignment confirms founder origin of all assets
6. **Management Control:** All business decisions made by qualifying individual
